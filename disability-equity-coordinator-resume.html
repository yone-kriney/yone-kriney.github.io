Professional ↩

Publishing Accessibility Fellow
at Princeton University Press, July 2023 – July 2024

  • Oversaw cross-departmental accessibility initiatives, advocacy, and working groups; wrote and implemented organizational accessibility policies and public accessibility statement.
  • Project management in digital production, including designing plans and timelines; status and accessibility reporting; direct document production and remediation; alt text writing; metadata management; vendor communication, documentation, and review.
  • Led internal accessibility training on web, word processor, video conferencing, and other digital publishing access best practices.
  • Directly fielded accessibility inquiries and requests from readers, authors, and libraries; modified publications in compliance within and beyond standards to address individual needs and concerns.
  • Read more about my work in Princeton University Press's feature on their 2023 Publishing Fellows

Accessible Digital Publishing Specialist
Freelance, May 2024 – present

  • Working directly with independent presses, artists, and journals to produce fully accessible digital publications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, InDesign, and other tools. Ensure compliance with WCAG and EPUB standards and best practices, with WCAG 2.2 AAA always the goal.
  • Provide detailed accessibility testing and reporting using manual review and accessibility testing software, ensure publications contain comprehensive and accurate metadata. Write alt text for images.
  • Advocate for the benefits of accessible digital media with publishers and educate on how to include accessibility in their default workflows.
